Dhanbad, Jharkhand

Location

Dhanbad is located in the Damodar River valley on the border of Jharkhand and West Bengal.

About

The city is called the 'Coal Capital of India' for housing one of the largest coal mines in the country. The prestigious institute, Indian School of Mines (now IIT Dhanbad) is situated in Dhanbad.

Weather

Dhanbad has a hot season from March to mid-July. The peak temperature in summer can reach 48 °C. In winter, the minimum temperature remains around 8 °C with a maximum of 25 °C. The average annual rainfall in Dhanbad is 1280 mm. The south-west monsoon lasts from July to October and the area gets more than 85% of the annual rainfall during these four monsoon months.

Population

The current metro area population of Dhanbad in 2023 is 1,390,000

Sports

Football and cricket are popular sports in Dhanbad

Things to do
